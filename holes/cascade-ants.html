Gate — AUTHORITY FAIL, and what it does and does not mean

20 paired seeds × 300 ticks, λ ∈ {0.1, 0.5, 1.0}: no real pattern differed from off by the preregistered held-out yield sign test. Controls sound — the sham (:cyber/baseline) tied off exactly on every seed at every λ, matching full event traces, and λ=0 tied for all five ids.

But only one of two channels was tested. The gate set :cyber-pattern {:id …} directly, so it never merged the patterns' @aif-delta into :aif-config. So the verdict reads “the hard-coded EFE terms do not move the ant” — not “the design patterns do not move the ant.” Channel 1 is untested and is the cheapest live question here.
