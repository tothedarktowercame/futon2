E-live-loop-1: what it wasn't doing — pinned to the R-contract

Companion to the R1–R18 wiring explainer. E-live-loop-1 asked why the cascade lane produces honest nothings at real cost (674 arena ticks, 0 cascade wins, every live fold an abstention). The answer is not one bug but six R-contract obligations left unwired at the policy grain — each pinned below with its evidence and the E-live-loop-2 microstep that wires it (bb futon2/scripts/live_loop_step.bb status for the live scoreboard). The mission lane closed R16 and fed R14 on 2026-07-02; the cascade lane got none of that — it is a limb the loop never learned to feel.

The six pins

R2 Observation was a stale summary, not the live tension

Wasn't: the lane's whole observation of a mission was ψ = title + status line — a ~368-byte banner, cached prose about a state, not the state. Thin ot in, size-1 cascade out is arithmetic, not fate.
Evidence: Flight 1 — rel-max 0.346, size 1, F +0.046 on the banner. S1 re-measured with sorry-grain ψ: rel up 12/12 (median 0.485), size median 4, five budget truncations — the lane's first ever. Ablation held. (p4ng/flights/)
Live-loop-2: sorry-grain ψ is the S1 result feeding all 2-series steps; the banner itself gets the status-refresh-before-work discipline so the cached observation is re-derived at every takeup.

R13 Policy-grain G existed but never entered selection

Wasn't: cascades carry their own valuation (wholeness, F) in the lane's currency, and the arena ranks in the 8-term G blend — the two currencies never meet. Every cascade entered the arena at a constant placeholder 0.0.
Evidence: 0 wins in 674 ticks; the argue-exhibit's cross-tables; the act-gate ANDs ΔF∧ΔG precisely so the bridge exists — but ΔG arrives nil without a construction (canon-fingerprint: ΔF −0.874 despite fold ΔG −0.25; bayesian: +1.049 ∧ −0.2 → pass).
Live-loop-2: the fold's ΔG is the designed bridge; steps 2d–2g give it something to carry (escrow records → replay seam → armed deposit → consumed on path, each gated).

R16 The loop-closure arc bypassed the only constructor with reach

Wasn't: two independent sufficient causes. The LLM fold — the only fold that constructs for contentful patterns (the classical rule table has 10 entries) — was unarmed (consent-correct) and had no live caller: close_loop.clj:30 tries rollout → classical → abstain. Feeding the escrow without the seam would have changed nothing.
Evidence: Flight 1 Leg 2 — classical abstain, LLM no-turn, realized nil, all honest, all empty; mock-armed LLM fold: 1 box, ΔG −0.5, closes? true. The no-live-caller finding is E-live-loop-1 finding 6 (S2 design pass).
Live-loop-2: 2e builds the replay seam dark (flag off, mock replay test, ΔG re-asserted on load); 2f/2g are ⚠ARMED — one real deposit, then live consumption, each on Joe's word, each gated.

R14 No precision over the lane — futility never fed back

Wasn't: R14's γ is live for the mission lane (fed by R16's realized-vs-expected since 2026-07-02) — but the cascade lane has no γ at all. 0-for-674 is an observation the system never updates on; the lane cannot lose precision however long it fails to pay.
Evidence: the constant-placeholder finding; the memoization note (waste is architectural, not thermodynamic — the appendage is maintained, never doubted).
Live-loop-2: 3b specifies and SIMULATES the γ-update rule against the 674-tick census (historical vs synthetic paying-lane trajectories must diverge sensibly) before any live wiring — the learned-γ thesis applied to the lane that motivated it.

R17 The policy repertoire was frozen — no niche for new patterns

Wasn't: three gates kept new patterns out of the policy space entirely: absent from the embeddings artifact (unretrievable), absent from the phylogeny (display-only, never choosable — cascade_construct.py:188-197), and priced at the unseen-pattern prior (2.398 nats ≈ an F-fine of 0.60 that seed coverage cannot pay). Structure learning at the pattern grain was structurally impossible.
Evidence: the three process-coherence seed patterns (the operator's own IF/HOWEVER/THENs) are currently invisible to the lane — stepper gates 2a/2b are honestly RED this morning; the 2.398-nat constant was cross-checked against S1's measured rare-singleton complexity.
Live-loop-2: 2a (embeddings), 2b (phylogeny), 2c (prior floor, probe attached as the gate) — the machine refuses 2c until 2a+2b are green.

R18 The system's own decision terms weren't observable

Wasn't: the EFE terms exist in the in-JVM trace (G-total, τ, policy-precision, G-pragmatic, G-epistemic) but are invisible to every agent tool — tick bodies unprojected into the evidence store. No reader can check real-vs-analogical claims about a quantity no reader can read; the faithfulness lens has nothing to look through.
Evidence: Flight 1 Finding 1 (tool-eye blindness), refined by the attempt-1 transcript: trace-visible in-JVM, agent-invisible everywhere else. Third independent arrival at the same seam that evening (Evidence Landscape; live-efe-map C5).
Live-loop-2: 3a makes the futility observable queryable by agent tools (with the wm-tick emitter upgrade, mistakes-ledger §13's open half); 3c routes lane-futility to the operator bulletin — stuck-means-signal at the WM grain.
