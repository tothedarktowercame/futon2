## HEAD (the one line)
**Recognize the symbolic acts already in the flow** (futonic 咅 articulation · 香 salience — perceive the structure an act already carries) and add a **thin explicit-symbol layer only for what recognition can't do** (override / disambiguation), so that sorries, goals, memes, corrections, and their edges are **minted at the point of creation**, into the same shapes the mining pipeline produces. Do as much as possible *very lightly*; stop breaking stride to process every turn with an agent twice.

## 0. The futon, doubled — the promise this mission makes good on
Two senses of *futon*, here **unified**:
- **atom of the future visible in the present** — an anticipatory **symbolic invariant**; a seed of what is coming, already available now.
- **蒲團** — the round cushion **woven** from rushes (蒲): scattered material drawn into a *concentrated* form (團 = 囗 around 專, the **spindle** — to concentrate) which, **when used, reproduces a concentrated state of mind** in its user (cf. `futon-theory/the-woven-form` — *"a woven, concentrated form that supports concentration"*; 坐破蒲團, sitting-through-the-cushion).

Unified, a **futon** is a symbolic invariant that **(i)** carries the future into the present, **(ii)** is *woven and concentrated* from scattered material, and **(iii)** *when re-used, reproduces the generative state that made it.* **Scatter → weave-and-concentrate → (in use) reproduce.**

The mapping to the work:
- **Futonic missions** already do the **atoms-of-future** half — they *gather* scattered material into named symbolic invariants (patterns · sorries-as-goals · the C-vector). And in that gathering they **verge on the 蒲團 half** — the weaving (mining = spinning the scattered into thread).
- **But the mission is scaffold** — structured, after-the-fact. The promise is only half-kept until the weaving also happens **in the flow**.

So **M-points-de-fuite is where the 蒲團 weaving happens on the line of flight.** Recognizing the symbolic acts (咅/香) **is** the weaving — drawing the scattered acts of live interaction into concentrated symbolic invariants *as they happen*. A futon minted in the flow is an atom-of-the-future (it grounds later work) *and* a concentrated woven form (re-encountering it reproduces its generative state). That is **making good on the promise of futon theory**: the name finally does **both** jobs — anticipation *and* concentration — in the live flow, not only the scaffold.

> **Derived criterion (for VERIFY, later):** a minted futon must be a **support**, not a log entry — re-encountering it must *re-induce* orientation (sense iii), or it is mere capture, not weaving.

## 1. IDENTIFY — shape-first

### The gap — stated as evidence · question · hypothesis
- **Evidence (what works, without doubt):** **Missions work.** They gather scattered material into working **symbolic invariants** (the atoms-of-future, woven-and-concentrated) and they reliably produce results — variably, but they *do* work. This is the **existence proof** that the *gather → weave → concentrate → support* mechanism is real and effective **at the mission scale, via scaffold.** And we have a second, costly route: the **heavy mining** (M-operational-vocabulary, M-goals-and-holes) *can* recover turn-level structure — but at real cost in **money and time** (the State apparatus).
- **Question (the gap — we genuinely do not know the answer):** **Can the same weaving be done at the TURN scale — in the flow, lightly — without the heavy scaffold of a mission doc and without the paid mining pass?** A mission is *itself woven from turns*; can the turns weave themselves *as they go*? **Unknown.** It is a real gap because missions work partly *because* they have structure (phase-spine, scopes, the operator authoring) — a single turn has far less. Whether the symbolic acts carry *enough light-recognizable structure in the flow* to weave a futon without that scaffold — or whether some minimum scaffold is irreducible — is exactly what we don't know.
- **Hypothesis:** **Yes — via recognition of symbolic acts.** The recognition-first language above (咅/香 + thin override symbols), which the futonic vocabulary has been **enunciating all along**: **間** (the interval/gap — the *between* where an act becomes legible, and where false-salience is the risk) · **香** (the salience that perceives it) · **鹽** (the generative composition that weaves and concentrates it). The micro-loop **間 → 香 → 鹽** is the turn-level form of what missions do at scaffold-scale. **The mining run is what makes the hypothesis testable now:** it taught us the empirical op-vocabulary and *which acts are recognizable lightly vs. need a model* — so from here we can attempt the light path instead of paying for the heavy one each time.

> **Falsifiable:** the hypothesis can lose — turns may not carry enough light-recognizable structure, and the scaffold/mining may prove irreducible. That is what MAP/DERIVE/VERIFY would test. This is why it is a question, not a plan.

### Sharpening (Joe, 2026-06-25): winding is not yet flight
A *turn* already evokes **winding** — clocking-in **winds the turn-thread onto the mission-spindle (專)**. So even a **robust autoclock-in** would let turns be woven directly into missions: the **蒲團 concentration**, and it is necessary. **But that is not the whole of it.** What we do with missions is not only gather and concentrate — it is to **give rise to lines of flight**; in many cases *the missions are the War Machine being built*. That generative side — the *fuite* proper, signs proliferating — is **obvious when the mining is animated** (the aggregate flow), and **looks quite different turn-by-turn / mission-by-mission** (the atomic view). So the mission holds **two registers** together:

| register | move | grain | reach |
|---|---|---|---|
| **winding** | turns → missions; the recognized act wound onto the spindle | atomic, per-turn | *light, mostly already within reach* (robust autoclock-in) |
| **flight** | the woven flow **gives rise to new directions** — the WM building itself | aggregate, **animated** | *the harder, deeper aim* — visible only in the flow |

**The gap, sharpened:** not merely *"can we wind turns into missions lightly,"* but **"can the light, in-the-flow weaving also *give rise to flight* the way the heavy scaffolded missions demonstrably do?"** Flight is a property of the **flow, not the atom** — so the recognized acts must **compose into a line**, not merely log individually. (That flight is *legible when animated* is itself a clue: the mechanism likely needs an aggregate/flow view, not only per-turn recognition.)

### The tension
Everything in the meme/goals-holes pipeline is **capture-after-the-fact** (the State apparatus): let the work flow past, then send a paid 70B over every turn — *twice* (forward → memes, backward → C-entries) — to *re-present* what flowed. This is why it is expensive, why ~50% came back as noise we spent a whole session gating, and why it "breaks stride." **The structure was never missing.** The agent knew it was minting a goal when it acted; it simply did not **declare** it — so we pay to guess it back, lossily.

The dual is **mint-at-the-point** (the line of flight): the structure flows out *with* the work, declared in a notation light enough not to interrupt it. Generative, not extractive. Declare-don't-guess taken to its limit: declare *as you create*, not reconstruct later.

### The shape
A **compact symbolic working-language** — a *set of roadsigns*, regularly revised, **explicitly NOT a fixed ontology** (Bateson, via Appendix F: the concepts are not to be "corrected and improved until at last they are worthy of a place in the list of fundamentals" — they are a working language). Its tokens:
- parse **deterministically** (a grammar, not a model) — that is the entire point;
- emit the **same record shapes** the pipeline emits (sorry · C-entry · meme/move · proof-edge) so they feed the **same downstream** (substrate-2, the C-vector, the PROOF layer) with zero re-mining;
- are terse enough to drop **inline, mid-turn**, without halting.

### Refinement (Joe, 2026-06-25): symbolic ACTS, not just symbols
The unit is not the symbol you type — it is the **symbolic act** already performed in the flow, *recognized* rather than imposed. This is futonic-logic's **咅 (articulation)**: *"the act of making a decomposition explicit — **not analysis (which presumes correctness) but perception of structure already present**,"* paired with **香 (embodied salience)**: a defeasible, agent-relative signal *"without full formalization."* So the design is a **gradient, recognition-first**:

| weight | mechanism | example | status |
|---|---|---|---|
| lightest | **recognition of the act** (咅/香) — the performative carries the force | *mentioning* a mission **is** the clock-in | **already done** — M-autoclock-in clocks into the first-mentioned mission |
| middle | light deterministic cue | "not X but Y" → a `correction`; a proposal → a `reach` | partly recognizable |
| heaviest | **explicit symbol, only as OVERRIDE** | `→ M-typed-holes` to *switch* mission (override the first-mention default) | the thin escape hatch |

**Principle: do as much as possible very lightly** — maximize recognition (perceive the structure the act already carries), add explicit symbols ONLY where recognition is insufficient or must be overridden. The heavy LLM-over-every-turn is the *last* resort, not the first; most of the work is 咅/香, not a parser and not a model.

Candidate primitives (to be DERIVEd — now read as the *override/escape-hatch* layer over the recognition layer):
- `→ M-typed-holes` — **switch** mission (override the recognized first-mention clock-in). The clock-in *itself* is already recognized, not typed.
- `?{…}` / `!{…}` / `~{…}` — explicit mint of a sorry / method-arrow / correction, **for when the act wasn't self-evidently one** — the recognizer catches the obvious cases; the symbol is for the rest.

### MAP finding (Joe, 2026-06-25): the recognizer is author-invariant — the operator and the Pilot are the same case
*"In-flow recognition of the act"* is the **same concept** for Joe's turns as for the **War Machine Pilot's** turns. The Pilot (`futon3c/holes/missions/M-war-machine-pilot.md`) is the agent that *inhabits and drives* the WM — it takes turns, authors, runs cycles, exactly as the operator does. So the recognizer is **author-invariant**: a turn is a turn; the symbolic act it performs (clock-in · reach · correction · mint) is recognized by the **same mechanism** regardless of who authored it.

This **collapses** the "non-anthropocentric attention" point from a *compose-across-three* into an **identity** — one recognizer, not three to reconcile. The author-axis only reappears one layer up:
- **Recognition (author-invariant):** *what act is this turn?* — shared by operator and Pilot.
- **Attribution (author-specific):** *whose act / whose belly?* — the provenance classifier (operator/agent/harness; the leak-fix) lives here. It matters for the **C-vector** (Joe's preferences) and ownership, **not** for the recognition. So the belly's operator-only discipline and this author-invariance are *not* in tension — they are two layers.

**The telos this exposes** (the Pilot's HEAD, quoting the Operator's Foreword): the WM Pilot is to be *"largely self-guiding, noticing its own points of tension, and repairing them."* **That noticing IS the recognizer.** So M-points-de-fuite's recognition layer — built once, author-invariant — is the very mechanism of WM self-guidance: it lets the **Pilot recognize its own acts in flight** (weave its own futons, give rise to its own lines of flight) exactly as it lets the operator's turns do. Operator and Pilot become **peers in the recognition layer**, and that peerage *is* "self-guiding." **M-points-de-fuite and M-war-machine-pilot meet here** — the same recognizer, pointed at either author's flow.
