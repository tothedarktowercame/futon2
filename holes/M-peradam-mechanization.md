## IDENTIFY — the tension

The ritual's integrity currently rests on social discipline (the
author chose blinding; the reviewer chose to score; the record chose
to carry the arming word). Mechanization must keep the integrity while
removing the ceremony: certificates issued by machinery, spendable as
reward, WITHOUT reopening Goodhart's door — the certificate must
remain refutable (the Pudding-Prover stance: evidenced by rejection).
