## 1. Findings banked (claude-10, 2026-06-27 ‚Äî verified, don't re-derive)

### 1a. Provenance: the *want* side is populated; the *paired arrow* is the empty join

- **`meme.db` (the `meme.arrow` store, futon3a) is the right SHAPE but under-populated.** `arrows` table = `source_id ‚Üí target_id` (a typed have‚Üíwant arrow), `mode ‚àà {construction, composition, analogy, untyped}`, `status ‚àà {open, correlated, constructed}` (the BHK/sorry lifecycle). **But it holds only 17 arrows / 24 entities / 0 proposals / 0 promotions** ‚Äî a seed/demo, *not* a mission-scale corpus.
- **M-goals-and-holes mined the C-vector (the WANT side), not paired arrows.** Per `goals-holes-readiness.html`: **263 derived C-entries** (stated 139 ¬∑ incompleteness 81 ¬∑ mess 43), each `{flavour, outcome-ref, preferred, weight, provenance, discharged-by, witness}`, in substrate-2 shape, **sim-only (0 :7071 writes)**. Producers: `futon6/scripts/c_vector.bb` (CPU, 3 channels) + `c_mine_joint.py` (Êáâ-voice GPU, stub-validated). A C-entry's `discharged-by` (the method that achieves the goal) points at **substrate-2's empty PROOF layer.**
- **The paired (have‚Üíwant) arrow = the forward‚Üîbackward JOIN = substrate-2's empty PROOF layer.** Not populated. Owned by [[M-populate-substrate-2]] (D4) + [[M-operational-vocabulary]]'s discharge side. This is *why* `meme.db` is empty of mission arrows ‚Äî the pairing was never built.
- **The only ACTUALLY-populated (have,want) pairs today: `futon6/data/diffsub-moves.edn` ‚Äî 55 moves**, each `:have "X/derive" :want "X/argue"` + `:move/class :close-hole` + `:delta-g`/`:score`/`:prior`. Pattern-PHASE granularity (transitions *within* a pattern ns), generated by `diffsub_emit.py` (JAX). Already consumed by the rollout ŒîG (`cascade_lane/rollout-g-for` filters on `:have`/`:want`).
- **Mission-level reconstruction material exists:** `futon6/data/mission-scope-trees/*.json` (e.g. `M-goals-and-holes.json`) ‚Äî `scope-count-by-binder-type` + `scope-hyperedges` (the Skolem scope structure). Not have/want directly; reconstructable.

**Net:** Joe's hypothesis is right *by design* (the `arrows` table is exactly a have‚Üíwant store) and unverified-*in-fact* for a real reason ‚Äî the paired corpus is the un-built PROOF join. We have **wants** (C-vector / 293 `:sorry` / star-map), **haves** (methods / diffsub), and **55 phase-grain pairs**; we do **not** have mission-level paired arrows.
