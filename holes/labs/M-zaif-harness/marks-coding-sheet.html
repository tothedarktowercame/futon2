RULES OF THE NOTATION

Use vs mention. A mark punctuating your statement is an event; a mark inside a description of the convention (“…with a ✘, for example”) is a mention and mints nothing.
The high-value keystroke is UPPERCASE ✓/✘ on agent work: (✓ :ref ft-… "…") — the :ref is what turns a judgment into a reward label.
Impact order when in doubt: ✓/✘ with :ref  ›  bare ✓/✘ on mission work  ›  ⚖  ›  ⚠  ›  💡  ›  the rest.
