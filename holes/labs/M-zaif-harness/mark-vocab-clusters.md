## Method

- **Pull:** all `type=coordination` evidence authored by `joe` from the futon1b store (:7073), deduplicated by `:evidence/id`.
- **Embed:** `sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2`, L2-normalized, batch size 64.
- **Cluster:** KMeans (`n_init=10`, seed=42) over k ∈ [8, 12, 16, 20]. k=12 selected by highest silhouette score (0.0251).
- **Terms:** per-cluster tf-idf (sublinear, English stopwords) where each cluster is treated as one document against the cluster-doc corpus.
- **Snippets:** 5 turns closest to the cluster centroid (cosine distance in normalized embedding space).
- **Mark-hit share:** fraction of turns in the cluster carrying a ✘/✓/💡 glyph or appearing in `b1-live-marks.edn`.
- **Determinism:** fixed seed throughout; checkpointed at pull (turns-cache.json) and embedding (embeddings-cache.npy).
