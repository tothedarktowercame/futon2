## The empirical warrant (why several cascades, not one better one)

Batch 7 proved this at the tactical grain, three times: two cascades folded
against the SAME mission reliably factorize its want-space into DISJOINT
clean surfaces (mechanism/scaffolding; shape/practice; the single-pattern
X-ray). A want is a projection; one projection hides structure that another
reveals. The same held immediately at the strategic grain: v0 (general
loop) cast M-cold-chain as "outward-facing boundary case, wave 1c"; the
stars-cascade casts it as **terminal s1 — the live instrument of the whole
T2 thesis**. Both are true; neither alone is the strategy.

## The merge discipline (from the fold conventions, transposed)

The merge is NOT an average and NOT a union-dump. Rules:

1. **Role-vectors, not role-overwrites.** A mission appearing in both
   cascades keeps BOTH roles, tagged by psi (same-move-different-aspect,
   held distinct — the batch-7 species rule). The merged box carries
   `:roles {:general … :stars …}`.
2. **Convergence points are the spine.** Missions/holes that BOTH wants
   rank highly are the merge's load-bearing set — cross-want confirmation,
   the strategic analog of C3/C4's honest-overlap convergence. First pass:
   the marks/labels lane (feeds both), M-cold-chain (wave-1c AND terminal),
   M-peradam-mechanization (grammar for both), **hole T2.5-conversion
   (v0 h2 ≡ stars sh2 — derived independently from both wants: the
   strongest hole-confirmation the method has produced)**.
3. **Tensions are kept, named, and priced.** Where the wants ORDER waves
   differently, the merge records the disagreement as an operator decision
   with its price: stars pulls livelihood/T2 and the Anthropic lane EARLIER
   than v0's instrumentation-first ordering; v0 answers that un-instrumented
   ascent can't certify its own rung-flips (anti-laundering needs the label
   contract). The merged wave-order therefore interleaves: instrumentation
   that CERTIFIES (wave 1, from v0) → rung-flips it can then witness
   (wave 2, from stars). That interleaving IS the merge's content.
4. **Terminals union; the psi composes.** The merged want is conjunctive
   prose, not a weighted sum — G-style scalarization happens (if ever) in
   the WM's scorecard, not in the plan document (no in-band formalism).
5. **Holes dedupe by derivation, not by wording**; a hole derived under
   both wants outranks every single-want hole in the mint queue.

Two reasons this isn't that claim: (a) the merge here is multi-OBJECTIVE

its best member on one yardstick; (b) the yardstick caveat cuts the other
way at this grain — strategy-cascades are evaluated by their revision
conditions firing or not (grounded, slow), which is closer to the grounded
peradam the verdict said to revisit under. If the merged cascade's waves
survive contact with reality longer than either parent's would have, that
is the falsifiable success reading; if v2 of the merge just IS one parent,
single-best held again and we say so.
